Tactile Image Maker

This device enables visually impaired individuals to perceive hand-drawn or computer-generated images and text through tactile feedback. It proves particularly beneficial for comprehending spatial layouts, such as maps

Overview

Easy and simple operation!
Just turn on the main unit and manually insert the special paper to create a tactile diagram.
Touch the shape with your finger
Even things that are difficult to convey with words alone can be easily shared by creating tactile diagrams.

Notes

  • Please be sure to dispose of unused capsule paper as combustible garbage.
  • Please do not send it for recycling. This will hinder the production of recycled paper.
  • Please use a multifunction device or laser printer to print on this product.

Recommendation

  • If you are using an inkjet printer, please let the copy dry for at least one day before inserting it into the 3D copy machine. (Recommendation)
  • Depending on the type of ink used (ink containing dye, etc.), it may not swell with PIAF.

FAQ

What are the characteristics?
It is characterized by allowing visually impaired people to easily visualize images and shapes by touching them. You can create a tactile diagram within 3 minutes after turning on the power.
What kind of users are you targeting?
Schools and workplace facilities for visually impaired people, etc.
What is the price?
90,000PHP＋(TAX)＋Shipping fee
Is there a discount?
We can negotiate depending on the number of units.
How do I use it?
Simply draw the diagram directly on the special paper or print it on a multifunction device, and then pass the paper through the main unit.
What advantages, advantages, and merits does it have?
One of the things that visually impaired people are not good at is understanding spaces that they cannot directly touch. This device is useful for tactile visualization of the overall layout of diagrams, etc.
What drawbacks, disadvantages, and disadvantages are there?
The difference in unevenness may be small due to the influence of the ambient temperature. However, it is possible to pass it through the main body as many times as you like until you reach the desired unevenness height.
What kind of printer prints on special paper and causes it to swell?
Please use a multifunction device or laser printer to copy onto this product. If you are using an inkjet printer, please let the copy dry for at least one day before inserting it into the 3D copy machine. (Recommendation) *Depending on the type of ink (ink containing dye, etc.), it may not inflate properly.
What should I do if the paper stops printing midway through printing?
Turn off the power to the main unit and slowly pull out the paper.
The main unit has become hot after continuous use. What should I do in this case?
If the main unit gets hot, it may not work even if paper is inserted. Please turn off the power to the main unit, wait for it to cool down, and then resume use.
