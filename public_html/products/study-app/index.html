Overview

Once the application is started, it is easy for visually impaired people to operate, and the questions are designed to not rely on visual information, allowing visually impaired people to study alone using Braille or audio. Not only people with low vision but also Braille users can read Braille faster by connecting it to our Braille display.

*When using on a computer, screen reader software (JAWS, NVDA) is required.
*Smartphone and tablet connectivity is currently under development. We are currently inquiring with (Google, Apple).

Applications specifically for visually impaired children
Many of the existing learning-related applications cannot be enlarged, read everything aloud, are complex to operate, and have problems that rely on visual information, making them easy for visually impaired children to use. There is nothing. This application aimed to be an application that allows visually impaired children to learn by themselves with a smartphone or computer.
Enjoy learning together
Feedback and notification functions such as learning status (time, correct answer rate), etc. are provided, and feedback is also provided to the student and his/her parents. Although this application is aimed at visually impaired people, sighted people can also enjoy learning together by looking at the same screen.
Abundant game functions
When you connect our braille display to a computer, smartphone, etc., you can enjoy a highly game-like menu for reading Braille by touch, which takes time, as well as games that you can enjoy by touching.
